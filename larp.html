  1. If A is a perumation matrix, does the transpose of A equal the inverse of A?
    Yes
  2. Describe the characteristics of a row-echelon form of a matrix.
    The number of leading zeros in each row is non-decreasing.
  3. Describe the characteristics of a reduced-row-echelon form of a matrix.
    • It is in a row-echelon form.
    • The leading non-zero entry is 1 in each row.
    • The elements
    • Each column containing a leading 1 has zeros in all its other entries.
  4. What are free variables and dependent variables?
    Columns without leading 1s in RREF of matrix (pivots) are free variables, and the rest are dependent variables (AKA pivot variables).
  5. What are two ways to solve system of equations?
    Elimination (efficient) and Determinants (inefficient).
